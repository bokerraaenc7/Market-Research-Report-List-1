<p><ul><li>Ruled Dispersive Element</li><li>Holographic Dispersive Element</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diffraction dispersive element market comprises primarily two types: ruled dispersive elements and holographic dispersive elements. Ruled dispersive elements utilize finely etched grooves to scatter light, which is effective for a wide range of applications, including spectroscopy. In contrast, holographic dispersive elements employ interference patterns recorded in a medium, allowing for higher efficiency and reduced stray light, making them ideal for advanced optical systems. Both types cater to diverse industries, including telecommunications, biomedical, and imaging technology.</p></p>
