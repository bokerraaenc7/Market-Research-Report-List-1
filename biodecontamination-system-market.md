<p><ul><li>Chamber Decontamination</li><li>Room Decontamination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biodecontamination system market is categorized into Chamber Decontamination and Room Decontamination. Chamber Decontamination involves using sealed environments to disinfect specific items or equipment, ensuring effective eradication of pathogens within a controlled space. Conversely, Room Decontamination encompasses the disinfection of entire spaces, typically utilizing methods like aerosolized biocides or vaporized hydrogen peroxide to eliminate contaminants from surfaces and air within larger areas. Both methods aim to enhance safety in healthcare, laboratories, and industrial settings.</p></p>
