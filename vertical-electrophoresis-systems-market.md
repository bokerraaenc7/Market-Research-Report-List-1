<p><p>The vertical electrophoresis systems market includes two primary types: agarose gel electrophoresis systems and polyacrylamide gel electrophoresis systems. Agarose gel electrophoresis is widely used for the separation of larger biomolecules such as DNA and RNA due to its easy preparation and resolution capabilities. In contrast, polyacrylamide gel electrophoresis systems offer higher resolution and are ideal for smaller proteins and peptides, making them essential in protein analysis and characterization. Both systems are critical in molecular biology and biochemistry research.</p></p>
