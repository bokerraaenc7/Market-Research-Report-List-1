<p><ul><li>Vertical Closed Loop</li><li>Horizontal Closed Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geothermal heat pump market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Vertical Closed Loop**: Deep boreholes are drilled to install vertical pipes, ideal for smaller spaces and areas with high soil conductivity. </p><p>2. **Horizontal Closed Loop**: Shallow trenches are dug to lay pipes horizontally, suitable for larger land areas and cost-effective. </p><p>3. **Open Loop**: Utilizes groundwater directly, circulating it through the system, which is efficient but dependent on water availability. </p><p>4. **Others**: This category encompasses innovative or hybrid systems improving efficiency and adaptability.</p></p>
