<p><p>The raster encoder market can be categorized into two main types: rotary encoders and straight edge encoders. Rotary encoders are used for measuring the rotational position or angle of an object, making them ideal for applications like motors and robotics. Straight edge encoders, on the other hand, measure linear displacement and are often employed in CNC machines and automated systems. Both types utilize optical or magnetic sensing technologies to provide precise feedback for motion control and positioning tasks.</p></p>
