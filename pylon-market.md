<p><ul><li>Tension Tower</li><li>Straight Tower</li><li>Corner Tower</li><li>Transposition Tower</li><li>Terminal Tower</li><li>Spanning Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pylon market types include various tower structures essential for overhead power transmission. Tension towers maintain line tension, while straight towers support horizontal spans. Corner towers redirect lines at angles, and transposition towers facilitate conductor phase shifting for balanced load distribution. Terminal towers mark the endpoints of transmission lines, whereas spanning towers bridge over obstacles. Each type plays a critical role in ensuring efficient and reliable power flow across distances, catering to specific engineering and infrastructural needs.</p></p>
